Beijing Riverside Garden Hotel


Beijing Riverside Garden Hotel is invested by Beijing Fengtai Science and Technology Park Construction Development Co.Ltd.As a five- star business conference hotel,it is under the management of BTG-jianguo Hotels & Rescorts which has the carte blanche.This hotel covers a gross area of 283hectares,with building area of 53,765 ㎡ it has main meeting place and break-out rooms of various sizes which jointly cover an area of 4000㎡. The location is very conveninet,three express ways (Beijing-Zhoukoudian Road,5th Ring Road ,and Lianshi Road),several municipal roads and subway No.14.Garden Show Park station is connected with the rail system which extends in all directions, It would be convenient to bring you at any Location in Beijing.This hotel has 283 suites in total ,which include presidential suite, ambassador suites,executive suite and deluxe standard rooms; the hotel provides Chineserestaurant , western restaurant, coffee house,lobby bar and recreational facilities.


This hotel has 283 elegant .fashionable ,capacious and comfortable premium rooms,including deluxe suite executive suite.humanized guest room for the disabled and various suites of different styles.High-speed broadband Internet access and WIFI are available in all guest rooms. Presidential suite is on the administration floor of the hotel with an area of 220 ㎡,which is capacious,comfortable,deluxe and elegant. All rooms are provided with ground glass window. One can enjoy a panoramic view of the city's beauty. Both interior design and decoration of the hotel have great originality.We use the "magic glass”,which appears in transparent status of indistinct status subject to your switch.it meets the dual requirements of permeability and privacy.The unique shower function highlights amenities of life and taste.

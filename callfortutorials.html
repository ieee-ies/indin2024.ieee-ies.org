Call for Tutorials

Tutorials

The conference will offer to all INDIN 2024 participants several high quality tutorials on the latest trends in Industrial Cyber Physical System, Industrial Electronics and industrial informatics.

Potential tutorial speakers are asked to submit a proposal up to two pages long, including:

  • title of the tutorial
  • the topics/content proposed for presentation, described through a narrative
  • outline schedule of delivery (headings) and expected duration
  • short biographies of all speakers.

Tutorial proposal submission

If you are interested in organizing a tutorial, please contact our conference email ( INDIN2024@gmail.com ) and send the proposal. The submission deadline is May. 03, 2024.

Successful tutorial proposers will be notified as soon as possible and will be asked to prepare and submit a PDF electronic version of their presentation materials. The conference organisers reserve the right to make the final decision of including the tutorial in the conference programme based on their final materials.
